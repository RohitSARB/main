The easiest example (real-world analogy)
Imagine a student taking an exam
📘 RAG = Open-book exam

Student is already smart (pretrained model)

You give them:

your notes

your textbook

your company docs

They read the notes during the exam

Notes can be updated anytime

👉 If the book changes, no retraining needed.

🧠 Fine-tuning = Training the student beforehand

You train the student repeatedly

They memorize patterns

They don’t get a book during the exam

If info changes → you must retrain

👉 Great for habits, bad for changing facts.
